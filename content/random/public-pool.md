The "Public Pool"

- 0.5oz of Malört
- 0.5oz of Amaro (Vecchio Amaro del Capo)
- 1.0oz of Spanish brandy
- Teaspoon of Triple Sec (Cointreau)
- Dash of bitters
- Ice, if desired

Bonus cocktail: The "Jacuzzi"

- 0.5oz of Malört
- 1oz of Tequila
- 1oz of Gin
- Tablespoon of simple syrup
- Dash of bitters
